About me

I am a Masters Student in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) at Mila and University of Montreal under the supervision of Professor Bang Liu and Professor Quoc Nguyen. My current interest centers around the intersection of machine learning and healthcare with a focus on the trustworthiness, fairness and equity of LLMs for clinicians. My other research interests revolves around Representation Learning, Mechanistic Interpretability, LLM Representation Engineering and AI4Science. This also led to me working as TA for the Representation Learning course this semester at Mila with Professor Aishwarya Agrawal and as a TA for the Machine Learning course at Polytechnique de Montreal with Professor Sarath Chandar.

Outside of academics, I am also a Data Scientist at Intact Insurance working in the DataLab in the Commercial Line Modeling team. I also hold the position of President of the UdeM AI student club where we organize events and workshops for students to learn more about AI and ML. In my freetime, I enjoy sports such as badminton, tennis and recently got into pickleball.
